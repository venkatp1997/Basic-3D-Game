Controls:- 
Use W,A,D,S to control the block.
    W-Move up in the camera direction
    S-Move down in the camera direction
    A-Rotate camera clockwise
    D-Rotate camera anti-clockwise
    Left Arrow-Move left by 1 unit
    Right Arrow-Move right by 1 unit
    Up Arrow-Move up by 1 unit
    Down Arrow-Move down by 1 unit
Helicopter View
    Click and drag to rotate clockwise and anticlockwise
    Use scroll-button to zoom in and out.
Use Q to quit the game.
